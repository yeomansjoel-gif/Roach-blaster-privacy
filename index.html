Privacy Policy for Roach Blaster

Last updated: February 2026

Roach Blaster is a casual mobile game developed by Axivon Tech, LLC. This privacy policy explains how the game handles data and what information is collected when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

Roach Blaster does not collect, store, or process any personal information from users. The game does not require account creation, login, email, name, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or device files.

The only data collected is through Google AdMob, which provides advertising services.

2. Advertising (AdMob)

Roach Blaster uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may automatically collect device information for the purpose of delivering personalized or non‑personalized ads, including:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device type
  • App interactions
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)

This data collection is handled by Google and governed by Google’s privacy policies.

3. In‑App Purchases

Roach Blaster offers optional in‑app purchases. All purchases are processed securely through Google Play Billing. Roach Blaster does not receive or store any payment information.

4. Children’s Privacy

Roach Blaster is intended for players ages 10 and up. The game does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Data Security

Roach Blaster does not store any personal data on servers. All gameplay data is stored locally on the user’s device.

6. Third‑Party Services

The app uses the following third‑party service:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising)

7. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
